CASINO RULES:

  • Decks: 6 decks are used (shuffled each hand).
  • Blackjack: Pays 3-to-2.
  • Splitting: Allowed on any pair. Double After Split (DAS) is allowed.
  • Dealer: Stands on ALL 17s (hard and soft).

BASIC STRATEGY:

Pairs:

  • Aces & 8s: Always SPLIT.
  • 10s & 5s: Never SPLIT.
  • 9s: SPLIT vs Dealer 2-6 & 8-9. STAND otherwise.
  • 7s: SPLIT vs Dealer 2-7. HIT otherwise.
  • 6s: SPLIT vs Dealer 2-6. HIT otherwise.
  • 4s: SPLIT vs Dealer 5-6. HIT otherwise.
  • 2s & 3s: SPLIT vs Dealer 2-7. HIT otherwise.
