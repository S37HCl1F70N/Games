CASINO RULES:

  • Decks: 6 decks are used (shuffled each hand).
  • Blackjack: Pays 3-to-2.
  • Push: A tie results in the bet being returned.
  • Dealer: Stands on ALL 17s (hard and soft).

BASIC STRATEGY:

This is a simplified guide to making the mathematically best decision.

Hard Hands (No Ace or Ace counts as 1):

  • 17-20: Always STAND.
  • 13-16: STAND if Dealer shows 2-6. HIT if Dealer shows 7-Ace.
  • 12: STAND if Dealer shows 4-6. HIT otherwise.
  • 11: Always DOUBLE DOWN.
  • 10: DOUBLE if Dealer shows 2-9. HIT otherwise.
  • 9: DOUBLE if Dealer shows 3-6. HIT otherwise.
  • 8 or less: Always HIT.

Soft Hands (Ace counts as 11):

  • Soft 19+ (A,8+): Always STAND.
  • Soft 18 (A,7): STAND vs Dealer 2,7,8. DOUBLE vs 3-6. HIT vs 9,10,A.
  • Soft 13-17 (A,2 - A,6): HIT, but DOUBLE vs Dealer 5-6.

Pairs:

  • Aces & 8s: Always SPLIT. (Note: Splitting not implemented in this version).
  • 10s & 5s: Never SPLIT.
